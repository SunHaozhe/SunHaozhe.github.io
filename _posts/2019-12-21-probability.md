# Gaussian distribution

One-dimensional case:

$$\frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi \sigma^2}} \exp(-\frac{(x-\mu)^2}{2\sigma^2})$$

$k$-dimensional case:

$$\frac{1}{\sqrt{(2\pi)^k \det \Sigma}} \exp(-\frac{1}{2}(x-\mu)^\intercal \Sigma^{-1} (x-\mu))$$
